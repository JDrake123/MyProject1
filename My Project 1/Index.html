THE BAND

We love Music

Formed in 2009, Blue Birds is a heavy metal band known for its powerful sound, emotional lyrics, and electrifying live performances. Starting out in America, the band began as a group of friends who shared a passion for creating meaningful music that connects with people of all ages. Over the years, we've performed in numerous venues from small clubs to large festivals sharing our energy and message of love, hope and rebellion. Our music blends rock, pop and jazz with our own unique style, creating a sound that's both fresh and familiar.
